Instructions:

1. If the number of clicks you have are odd, you can only click odd numbered lightbulbs to advance. Same goes for even clicks and even lightbulbs

2. If you click an odd light bulb on an even click the game will reset

3. Default colors are Orange. After 1 successful click the lightbulb turns purple. After 2 successful clicks the lightbulb turns pink.

The objective of the game is to get all lightbulbs to turn pink without resetting.
